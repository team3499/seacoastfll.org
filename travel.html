Getting to the Event

All tournament functions take place at the Memorial Union Building (MUB) located near downtown Durham on the beautiful campus of the University of New Hampshire.

Memorial Union Building (MUB)
83 Main Street
Durham, NH 03824

Drivers unfamiliar with the area are encouraged to approach Durham from the west via Route 155A to avoid the downtown Durham loop and simplify access to the MUB drop-off entrance.

Local Attractions

Need some good coffee? The local businesses of downtown Durham are only a short walk away.

From The West

  • Route 101 East to Exit 7
  • Route 125 North to the Lee Traffic Circle
  • Route 4 East (2 miles) to Route 155A Exit
  • Route 155A East to UNH

From I-95 North

  • I-95 North to Exit 4 (left exit)
  • Spaulding Turnpike (16N) to Exit 6W
  • Route 4 West to Route 155A Exit
  • Route 155A East to UNH

From I-95 South

  • I-95 South to Exit 5
  • Spaulding Turnpike (16N) to Exit 6W
  • Route 4 West to Route 155A Exit
  • Route 155A East to UNH

Once on Route 155A (Main Street, Durham), proceed east for 1.5 miles continuing through the traffic circle and past the stadium, lacrosse fields, and Whittemore Center. As you enter the main campus, take the first right after the intersection of Garrison Avenue and veer left up the driveway to the Memorial Union Building. This is the drop-off location for equipment and team members.

Parking

Parking for tournament attendees is free at B Lot on campus. To get there after dropping off your team and equipment:

  • Turn right leaving the MUB drop-off entrance onto Main Street
  • Proceed southeast on Main Street for 0.2 miles into downtown Durham
  • Turn right on Mill Road
  • Proceed southwest on Mill Road for 0.3 miles to Academic Way
  • Turn right on Academic Way
  • B Lot is on your left

The MUB is only a short walk across campus. Walk further into campus along Academic Way then follow the foot paths on the west side of Randall Hall. The entrance to the south end of the MUB is along the elevated walkway. Once inside, proceed up the stairs to the third floor to reunite with your team.
