Welcome Teams

We are pleased to welcome a full roster of twenty FLL teams to the 2012 Seacoast FLL Qualifying Tournament!

On Saturday, November 10th, twenty FIRST LEGO League teams comprised of over 150 students, 30 team mentors, and countless volunteers will gather at the Memorial Union Building on the beautiful campus of the University of New Hampshire for the 2012 Seacoast FLL Qualifying Tournament.

Running in parallel to the FLL events, we have over a dozen JFLL teams competing this year!

Starting a Team

Are you interested in learning more about FIRST LEGO League and possibly starting a team? A workshop for adults considering starting up an FLL team will be held during the tournament at 1:00 PM. The workshop is free, but please contact carolyn@seacoastfll.org to register.
