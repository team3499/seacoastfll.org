Welcome Teams

We are pleased to welcome a full roster of twenty FLL teams to the 2013 Seacoast FLL Qualifying Tournament!

On Saturday, November 9th, fifteen FIRST LEGO League teams comprised of over 100 students, 20 team mentors, and countless volunteers will gather at the Memorial Union Building on the beautiful campus of the University of New Hampshire for the 2013 Seacoast FLL Qualifying Tournament.

Running in parallel to the FLL events, we have over a dozen JFLL teams competing this year!
