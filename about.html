About Us

The Seacoast FLL Qualifying Tournament is an officially sanctioned FIRST LEGO League (FLL) qualifying tournament organized by the FLL regional operational partner, BAE Systems. FLL qualifying tournaments, or qualifiers, follow a similar format to FLL championship tournaments abiding by FLL standards in judging, awards and overall quality.
